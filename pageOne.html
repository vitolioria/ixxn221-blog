Introduction to IXXN 221

Understanding the course and setting personal designer goals

During the first week, we went through brief introductions from both course tutors as well as with other students taking the course.

Was able to get a course overview which included brief examples of what to expect from assignments and what the outcome should generally be like.

Although it was a quick class, I think it is important to set personal goals while preparing for the course.

As I aim to become a UX designer, UX design involves understanding user needs, organizing information, testing designs, and making them user-friendly for the intended audience.

Because of this, I think it is important to try and stay on top of my work so that, not only am I able to keep up with other courses but to also spend more time submitting assignments that are up to standard and become more understanding of UX practices etc.
